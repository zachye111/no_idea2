Ironman

2008's Iron Man tells the story of Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who is kidnapped and forced to build a devastating weapon. Instead, using his intelligence and ingenuity, Tony builds a high-tech suit of armor and escapes captivity.

WandaVision

WandaVision's basic setup finds Wanda and Vision living in a sitcom-style suburban world as a married couple with two children. Each episode (at least those featured in the various trailers) is modeled after sitcoms from different eras (I Love Lucy and I Dream Of Jeannie among them).
